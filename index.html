Welcome to The Garden Rage Game!


With your characters 'Jim' and 'Bob', you battle it out to see who becomes the Head Gardener. Using the arrow keys, your player is allowed 3 moves vertically or horizontally, picking up weapons on the way. When the players are next to each other the battle can begin and you can choose to attack or defend. Each weapon causes different amounts of damage, but with 'Defend' mode on, the damage can be reduced by 50% on the next attack.


Have fun!


Press 'S' to start.
